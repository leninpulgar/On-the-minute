Meet the Team

Valentina Rodríguez

Valentina has been involved in fitness since her early years passing through several sports. After graduating, Valentina joined the crossfit community where she met her Coach and now husband Coach Mono. 2 pregnancies and a couple of years later, she strongly believes the human body can achieve incredible and positive results when it is well guided and constantly challenged.

Founder
Gerardo Molina

Coach Mono started his career at the early age of 20, as an assistant coach for Van Nuys high school (Ca) soccer junior varsity team. With a background in several sports and strong leadership skills, he was able to become Head Coach by the age of 22 and winning the first ever city championship for that high school. Moving to Florida, years later, he was introduced to Crossfit. It became a passion and then a new career. Coaching, teaching, and helping others achieve their fitness goals has made him start from a volunteer assistant trainer to co-owner of Dafactory Training Facility. In the last 4 years, programming workouts has been his main focus. Making them effective, creative, and improving athlete’s quality of life. Thru his career as a coach he has train over 5 pregnant women all with beautiful strong and healthy kids now, including his wife 2 pregnancies where she competed locally and trained until 2 days prior to giving birth.

Founder
Tania Bocaranda

After playing various sports during high school, Tania found her true passion in fitness when she started Crossfit in 2013. Following her competitive side, she challenged herself through local competitions where she gained knowledge and experience. She was given the opportunity to start as an assistant coach, where she was in charge of the onboarding of new members as well as helping out the head coach during class. Tania has been consistently training and coaching for the past 4 years and lately started getting involved in the programming side of fitness.
